About Us

Founders Eve and Mike saw how children were addicted to technology and how it was distracting them from their studies. They knew that there had to be some way for them to use technology as an aid to help children learn. Their passion for technology and education lead them to make Wrist Warriors, a smartwatch focused on helping children build good habits.

What is Wrist Warriors?

Wrist Warriors combines the latest studies on child psychology and development with smart watches in order to instill positive habits in children. The Wirst Warrior is a child friendly product that rewards children who can achiever their goals through the concept of gamification. With Wrist Warrior, Children can be rewarded for doing their homework, chores, or anything else that needs to be done. It's the perfect product for parents that want their kids to have fun doing important things in life!
